About

My name is Kelsey Taylor.

A Full-time Mom turned Software Developer turned Data Analyst. Now I consider myself all three.

I fell in love with programming when I took the 8-Week Tech-Mom's course November 2021. From there I dove into Web Development and completed my own personal sites and a website for a small-business client.

That's when Data Analytics found me.

I accepted the Women in Engineering Scholarship to attend Devmountain's Full-time 16-Week Coding & Data Analytics Bootcamp. I graduated December 2022. I am currently an Analytics Engineer with the company Health Catalyst. I believe in making data-driven decisions, and have a knack for making beautiful Analytics Visualizations. I love working with SQL and tools such as Tableau and Power BI.
